## 1. General Inquiries

If you require assistance, you may reach out to us at **hi@solitar.link**. To help us assist you efficiently, please adhere to the following guidelines:

- **Language:** All correspondence must be written in English.
- **Advertising:** Solitar does not accept advertisements. Promotional inquiries will be ignored.
- **Mailing Lists:** We do not operate any email mailing lists. If you receive spam containing our shortened links, please do not send an unsubscribe request; instead, use our Abuse Reporting procedure below.
- **Clarity:** To ensure a prompt response, please ensure your email is clear, concise, and directly related to Solitar's services.

## 2. Reporting Abuse

We take platform abuse seriously. If you encounter a Solitar shortened URL that violates our Terms and Conditions, please report it at at **abuse@solitar.link**.
