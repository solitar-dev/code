At Solitar, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle data to provide a secure and efficient URL shortening service.

## 1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifying information (PII) from our users. We only collect and store the data strictly necessary to operate and protect our service. This includes:

- **Operational Data:** The original long URLs submitted for shortening.
- **Security Data:** Information necessary to identify, track, and prevent abuse of our platform, such as IP addresses.
- **Aggregate Statistics:** Non-identifying data used to compile statistics on how individual links are utilized, including browser types and countries of origin.

## 2. Web Analytics

To understand site usage and improve our platform, we use **Cloudflare Web Analytics**. Unlike traditional analytics platforms, Cloudflare Web Analytics does not use client-side tracking cookies or collect personally identifiable information. It is a privacy-first solution designed purely to monitor site performance and overall visitor interactions without tracking individual users.

## 3. Information Sharing and Security

We do not sell or share your data for marketing purposes.

## 4. Link Privacy

Please be aware that URLs shortened by Solitar are **not private** and should not be treated as secure. Because shortened URLs are relatively short, third parties could potentially guess the link destination. We strongly advise against using Solitar to shorten links that point to sensitive, confidential, or secure personal data.

## 5. Public Statistics

To maintain transparency, anonymous statistics regarding the shortened URLs you create (such as the creation date and the total number of visits) are not treated as private. This statistical data is publicly accessible to anyone utilizing the site.
